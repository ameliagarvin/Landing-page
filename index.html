About me

Amelia Garvin is a Vancouver based multi-disciplinary artist working in writing, visual and sculptural mediums. Her work has been exhibited in various group shows in Vancouver and online. Her work capture her ideas of youth, the body and nostalgia. Garvin holds a BFA from Emily Carr University currently working as a freelance illustrator and graphic designer.
